Our Story

FacePe tackles identity.

In 2022, we found a flaw: payment systems verified cards, not people. We created a biometric layer. Today, FacePe ensures fast verification.

2022

Founded to fill a gap in payment systems: verifying people, not just instruments. Seed funding closed.

2023

First prototype: 3D camera + AI compute. Bank pilot launched. 99% accuracy in real-world.

2024

Series A closed. Retail self-checkout launched. AI Avatar drive-thru deployed. 1,000+ terminals live.

2025

Expanding network. Onboarding launched. 100K+ transactions. Building identity layer for commerce.
