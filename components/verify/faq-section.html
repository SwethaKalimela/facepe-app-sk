Is my face data safe with FacePe Verify?

Yes. Biometric templates are encrypted and stored locally on-device. FacePe Verify uses 3D liveness detection and is SOC 2, PCI-DSS, and GDPR compliant.

How fast is verification compared to manual KYC?

Most verifications complete in under 5 seconds — replacing 5–15 minute manual branch workflows.

What hardware is required at the branch?

FacePe Verify kiosks include 3D depth cameras and IR sensors. Installation typically takes under one hour per device.

Does FacePe integrate with existing core banking systems?

Yes. REST APIs connect verification events to your KYC, CRM, and teller notification systems.

Can we run a pilot before full rollout?

Yes. Schedule a pilot to deploy at one or more branches and measure verification time, fraud reduction, and customer satisfaction.
