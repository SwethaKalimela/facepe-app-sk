Is my face data safe with FacePe?

Yes. Your face is converted to an encrypted biometric template stored locally on your device — never on our servers. We use 3D liveness detection to prevent spoofing, and our infrastructure is SOC 2, PCI-DSS, and GDPR compliant.

How fast is FacePe checkout compared to cards?

FacePe Online completes identity verification and payment authorization in under 2 seconds — faster than OTP entry, CVV typing, or mobile wallet unlock on the web.

Does FacePe work without internet?

The Web SDK requires connectivity for payment authorization. Mobile SDKs cache enrolled templates for brief offline windows and sync when the connection returns.

Which merchants support FacePe today?

FacePe Online is live with ecommerce, D2C, and food delivery pilots. Contact us for the current merchant map and integration timeline for your stack.

Can I delete my FacePe data at any time?

Yes. Users can delete enrollment and biometric templates from the FacePe app. Merchants receive audit confirmation when deletion requests are processed.

What devices do I need as a merchant?

No hardware required. Drop the Web SDK into your checkout page or use our iOS and Android SDKs. Works with any device camera that supports 3D liveness.
