Is my face data safe with FacePe?

Yes. Your face is converted to an encrypted biometric template stored locally on your device — never on our servers. We use 3D liveness detection to prevent spoofing, and our infrastructure is SOC 2, PCI-DSS, and GDPR compliant.

How fast is FacePe checkout compared to cards?

Average FacePe checkout completes in under 2 seconds — faster than card tap, chip insert, or mobile wallet unlock at self-checkout lanes.

Does FacePe work without internet?

Terminals cache enrolled templates for offline verification. Transactions sync when connectivity returns — no lane downtime during brief outages.

Which merchants support FacePe today?

FacePe is live in grocery, big-box retail, and convenience pilots across North America. Contact us for the current merchant map in your region.

Can I delete my FacePe data at any time?

Yes. Users can delete enrollment and biometric templates from the FacePe app. Merchants receive audit confirmation when deletion requests are processed.

What devices do I need as a merchant?

FacePe supplies 3D camera modules and lane terminals that integrate with NCR, Toshiba, Diebold, and Verifone POS systems — we handle install and configuration.
