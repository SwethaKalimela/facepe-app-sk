Is my face data safe with FacePe?

Yes. Your face is converted to an encrypted biometric template stored locally on your device — never on our servers. We use 3D liveness detection to prevent spoofing, and our infrastructure is SOC 2, PCI-DSS, and GDPR compliant.

How fast is FacePe checkout compared to cards?

FacePe AI Avatar completes recognition and payment in under 2 seconds at the drive-thru window — faster than card tap, chip insert, or mobile wallet unlock.

Does FacePe work without internet?

Lane terminals require connectivity for payment authorization. Customer templates cache locally for brief offline recognition; transactions sync when the connection returns.

Which merchants support FacePe today?

FacePe AI Avatar is live with QSR and drive-thru pilots across North America. Contact us for the current merchant map and rollout timeline for your brand.

Can I delete my FacePe data at any time?

Yes. Users can delete enrollment and biometric templates from the FacePe app. Operators receive audit confirmation when deletion requests are processed.

What devices do I need as a merchant?

FacePe supplies 3D depth cameras and lane hardware that integrate with major QSR POS systems — we handle install, calibration, and configuration per lane.
