Is my face data safe with FacePe?

Yes. Your face is converted to an encrypted biometric template stored locally on your device — never on our servers. We use 3D liveness detection to prevent spoofing, and our infrastructure is SOC 2, PCI-DSS, and GDPR compliant.

How fast is FacePe checkout compared to cards?

Most FacePe checkouts complete in under 2 seconds — faster than chip-and-PIN or mobile wallet flows.

Does FacePe work without internet?

Edge verification works offline for enrolled users; settlement syncs when connectivity returns.

Which merchants support FacePe today?

FacePe is live in retail, banking, QSR, and ecommerce pilots across North America and APAC.

Can I delete my FacePe data at any time?

Yes. You can delete your biometric template and account data from the app at any time.

What devices do I need as a merchant?

Any modern camera-equipped POS, kiosk, or mobile device with our SDK — no proprietary hardware required.
