Why FacePe Wins

Every other method has a flaw. FacePe doesn’t.

Cards, QR codes, and mobile wallets all verify the payment tool — not the person using it. FacePe verifies you.

Cards verify the token, not you

Anyone with your card can use it. Theft and fraud are part of the system.

  • FacePe verifies the person, not a token.
  • No physical item to lose, steal, or clone.
  • Works even if your wallet is lost or stolen.

QR codes are just digital cards

Screenshot a QR code to pay? Scanning is clumsy, slow-lighting, and security matters.

  • No scanning, no fumbling, no lighting issues.
  • Your face cannot be screenshotted or shared.
  • 50% faster than a checkout flow.

Wallet still need your phone present

Tap to pay is quick, but if your battery dies or the nfc fails, you rely on a device.

  • Works instantly at any terminal — no device needed.
  • Your face doesn’t run out of battery or lose signal.
  • Same sub-2-second speed — device-free.
