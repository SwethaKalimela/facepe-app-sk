# Brand Guidelines (FacePe)

## Positioning

FacePe is the **identity layer for payments**: biometric verification, device-free checkout, and merchant-facing trust—**not** “another card form.” Copy and visuals consistently reinforce **person > instrument**.

## Personality (from live Figma marketing)

| Trait | How it shows up |
|--------|------------------|
| **Premium** | Generous whitespace (`1240px` content, `60px` section padding), restrained shadows, pill CTAs |
| **Fintech** | Stats (“2s”, “60%”, “99.8%”), comparison patterns, compliance hints (SOC 2, PCI-DSS, GDPR in FAQ) |
| **Minimal** | White fields, hairline borders, sparse color (mostly ink + one purple system) |
| **Enterprise** | Structured nav (“Product”, “Solution”, “Enterprise”), dense footer IA, “Contact us” + “Get Started” |
| **Futuristic** | 3D face / device renders, dashed “scanner” frames, subtle grid lines + star accents (decorative only) |
| **Trust-focused** | FAQ-first objections, testimonial with role + company, “Real Deployments” style proof sections |

## Visual language

- **Typography-led heroes** with one accent color (`#5f15ee`) for proof points—not rainbow gradients on text.
- **Single brand gradient** reserved for **primary actions** and small highlights (`#9c6cfe` → `#5000ea`).
- **Soft lilac fields** (`#f9f6ff`, `#f0ebff`) for “tech” surfaces; keep **body areas white** for readability.
- **Decorative motif:** thin orthogonal lines + small stars—optional; **never** required for comprehension (omit on mobile if busy).

## Voice & tone (inferred from UI strings)

- Short, declarative headlines (“Pay with Your Identity. Not Your Wallet.”).
- Mix **speed** and **security** claims with measurable numbers when possible.
- **Avoid** playful jargon; prefer operational clarity (“Verified in 0.3s”, “schedule a Pilot”).

## Inspiration map

Stripe / Linear / Brex / Ramp: **whitespace**, **single accent**, **crisp type**, **one hero CTA pair** max above the fold.

## Non-negotiables

- Do **not** add extra accent colors or second gradients for decoration.
- Do **not** animate background grids or stars.
- Do **not** reduce contrast on body copy for style.
